3. USB Drivers & Setup

Connection & Driver Disclaimer

To successfully program your ESP32 board directly from the browser, your computer needs the correct USB-to-UART bridge drivers installed. Without these drivers, your system will not recognize the COM port of your device.

If your device is not detected when you click "Install Firmware", please download and install the driver corresponding to your board's USB chip below, then restart your browser.

CH340 / CH341 Driver Common for low-cost ESP32 / CYD boards (Windows EXE) CP210x VCP Drivers Common for official NodeMCU & Espressif kits
